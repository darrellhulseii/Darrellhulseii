We're Hiring: Outside Sales Representative (1099 Contractor)

We are seeking motivated, independent Outside Sales Representatives to join our team as 1099 contractors. As an authorized ADT dealer, you'll sell smart home security systems and help protect families in our community.

Job Description

As an Outside Sales Representative, you will conduct door-to-door sales (with provided leads – no cold calling required), present ADT-monitored security systems, and close deals directly with customers. Responsibilities include:

  • Prospecting and engaging potential customers in residential areas
  • Conducting security needs assessments and product demonstrations
  • Closing sales and processing customer agreements
  • Maintaining accurate records of sales activities
  • Following up with leads and ensuring customer satisfaction

Expectations of the Role

We expect self-motivated contractors who thrive in a performance-based environment. Key expectations:

  • Meet or exceed sales targets (top performers close 4–6 accounts per week)
  • Maintain professional demeanor and build customer trust
  • Work flexible hours, including evenings and weekends
  • Pass background check and have reliable transportation
  • Adhere to ADT dealer guidelines and ethical sales practices
  • Participate in ongoing training (D2DU 2.0 / Knockstar University)

Compensation (1099 Commission-Only)

Average Earnings: $60,000 – $120,000/year
Top Performers: $150,000+ annually
Commission per closed account: ~$500 (paid weekly)

Compensation is 100% commission-based (W-9/1099). Earnings vary by performance, location, and volume. Estimates based on industry data from D2DU 2.0, Knockstar University, and ADT authorized dealer reports.
